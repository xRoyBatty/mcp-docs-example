Present Perfect: Life Experiences

Level: B1 Skills: Grammar, Speaking Time: 30 minutes

Instructions

In this worksheet, we'll practice using the Present Perfect tense to talk about life experiences. Pay attention to the form: have/has + past participle.

Exercise 1: Fill in the Blanks

Complete the sentences using the correct form of the Present Perfect.

1. Sarah five countries this year.

2. They sushi before.

3. James in Paris for three years.

4. We our homework.

5. The teacher our tests.

Remember: Use 'has' for he/she/it and 'have' for I/you/we/they.

Exercise 2: Multiple Choice

Choose the correct form of the verb.

1. Maria _____ to Australia twice.

2. They _____ their lunch yet.

Exercise 3: Matching

Match the beginnings with the correct endings to make true Present Perfect sentences.

I have never
seen snow before.
She has just
arrived home.
They have already
done their work.

Exercise 4: Speaking Practice

Record yourself answering these questions using the Present Perfect.

1. Have you ever traveled abroad?

2. What new foods have you tried recently?

3. Have you learned any new skills this year?

Answer Key
