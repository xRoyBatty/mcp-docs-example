Present Perfect: Life Experiences

Level: B1 Skills: Grammar, Speaking Time: 30 minutes

Instructions

In this worksheet, we'll practice using the Present Perfect tense to talk about life experiences. Pay attention to the form: have/has + past participle. Remember to use 'has' with he, she, and it, and 'have' with I, you, we, and they.

Exercise 1: Fill in the Blanks

Complete the sentences using the correct form of the Present Perfect.

1. Sarah five countries this year.

2. They sushi before.

3. James in Paris for three years.

4. We our homework.

5. The teacher our tests.

Remember: Use 'has' for he/she/it and 'have' for I/you/we/they.

Exercise 2: Multiple Choice

Choose the correct form of the verb.

1. Maria _____ to Australia twice.

2. They _____ their lunch yet.

Exercise 3: Matching

Match the sentence beginnings with their correct endings by dragging and dropping.

They have just
moved into a new house.
She has never
been to Paris before.
We have already
finished our homework.
He has recently
started learning Japanese.

Exercise 4: Speaking Practice

Listen to these questions and record your answers using the Present Perfect.

1. Have you ever traveled abroad? Where have you been?

2. What new foods have you tried recently?

3. Have you learned any new skills this year?
