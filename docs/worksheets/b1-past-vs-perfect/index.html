Past Simple vs Present Perfect

Level: B1 Time: 20 minutes Skills: Grammar

Instructions

This worksheet will help you understand when to use Past Simple and when to use Present Perfect. Pay attention to time expressions and whether the action is finished or connected to the present.

1. Choose the Correct Tense

Select the correct form of the verb for each sentence.

I _____ to Paris three times in my life.

She _____ her phone yesterday morning.

2. Complete the Sentences

Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb in brackets.
1. John (never/eat) sushi before.
2. Last summer, we (visit) Rome for a week.
3. I (live) in this city since 2015.

3. Match Time Expressions

Match each sentence with its correct time expression.
I saw that movie
They have been to Australia
We have lived here
since 2010
yesterday evening
never before
